🏛️ Regulatory Compliance & Banking Policies

Citadel National Bank operates under the strict supervision of the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) and adheres to all applicable banking regulations.

🏛️

RBI Regulated

Licensed under the Banking Regulation Act, 1949. Governed by RBI Master Directions on Interest Rate, KYC Norms, and Cyber Security Framework.

🛡️

DICGC Insurance

All deposits insured up to ₹5,00,000 per depositor under the Deposit Insurance & Credit Guarantee Corporation (DICGC), a subsidiary of RBI.

📋

KYC/AML Compliance

Compliant with PMLA Act 2002 and RBI's Master Direction on KYC. Customer identity verification is mandatory as per regulatory requirements.

🔒

IT Act 2000 & CERT-In

Compliant with Information Technology Act, 2000 and CERT-In guidelines for incident reporting. All systems follow RBI's Cyber Security Framework.

📊

DPSI Act 2023

Adheres to the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. Customer data is processed only for lawful purposes with appropriate consent.

⚖️

Banking Ombudsman

Customers can escalate unresolved grievances to the RBI Banking Ombudsman under the Integrated Ombudsman Scheme, 2021.

💳

RBI Tokenisation

Card-on-file tokenisation as per RBI circular dated 07.09.2021. No actual card data stored — only encrypted tokens.

📱

NPCI & UPI Guidelines

Registered with National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI). All digital transactions comply with UPI & IMPS safety protocols.
